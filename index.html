For the 2024 scholarship, students who have passed Class 12 from the Jammu and Kashmir board or the CBSE board in Jammu during the 2021-22 and 2022-23 academic years are eligible to apply.

Students can check the status of their scholarship application by logging into the official PMSSS or AICTE portal using their application credentials. Updates are usually posted in the applicant's dashboard or application status section.

Scholarship recipients are selected based on their academic performance in Class 12, their domicile status, and the availability of seats in eligible undergraduate courses. The selection process may also consider other factors such as financial need and merit.

If your application is rejected, you can appeal or resolve issues by contacting the scholarship helpline or support center provided by theAICTE or Jammu & Kashmir Government. Additionally, you can submit a formal grievance or appeal through the official PMSSS portal, providing all relevant details and supporting documents.
